Supercell dodged ‘the great resignation’, but it’s
doubling down on staff wellbeing anyway

“We work with a company who did a whole bunch of training to
increase awareness of mental health and also reduce the stigma,”
says Mansford. “If people aren’t talking about it, then that’s a
problem in itself.” Staff can ask to be trained like mental health first
aiders under a program called ‘Jedis of the mind’, and in Helsinki
there’s a psychologist on-site twice a week. There are a raft of other
training and online resources for staff who want to learn more in a
less public way as well.
